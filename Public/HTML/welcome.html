Rules of the game :-

**********************************
1)You will Take chances in Alternate Manner.(Player 1 will always start). 
!!!! Look Out for the timer dont lose your chance!!!!

2)The tic tac toe consist of a 3x3 checkbox grid in each slot.

3)According to the Input in Checkbox grid the next player is restricted to the respective
  bigger slot.

4)To win you have to capture a checkbox with the simple rules of tic tac toe in that checkbox.

5)Once the a checkbox is captured it is reserved for the winner.

6)Win 3 Checkboxes in order to win the game.

7)If by turns the player reaches a already won checkbox, A "Universal Input" will be provide 
  to continue any where in the slots.
